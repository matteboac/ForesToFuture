Causes of Deforestation

Learn how agriculture, logging, and urban expansion contribute to forest loss.

Deforestation is primarily driven by agricultural expansion, illegal logging, and urban development. Forests are cleared for crops, livestock, and infrastructure, leading to habitat loss, soil erosion, and increased greenhouse gas emissions.

Effects on Wildlife & Ecosystems

Discover how habitat loss and deforestation threaten countless species and ecosystems.

Deforestation fragments habitats, disrupts food chains, and pushes species to extinction. Ecosystem services such as pollination, water purification, and carbon sequestration are diminished, impacting both wildlife and humans.

Solutions & Conservation Efforts

Explore reforestation, sustainable logging, and community initiatives to protect forests.

Reforestation programs, sustainable forest management, and community-led conservation projects help restore ecosystems. Encouraging sustainable agriculture and enforcing anti-logging laws are crucial steps in mitigating deforestation.
