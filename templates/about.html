About This Project

Discover the mission behind ForesToFuture and how we protect forests, biodiversity, and the climate.

I. General Objective

The primary objective of ForesToFuture is to raise nationwide awareness about Deforestation and Biodiversity Loss in the Philippines. This project aligns strongly with two major Sustainable Development Goals: SDG 13 – Climate Action and SDG 15 – Life on Land.

The platform aims to educate the public on how forest loss contributes to climate change, endangers wildlife, and threatens ecosystem stability. By providing accessible digital information, this initiative empowers communities—especially the younger generation—to take active roles in environmental stewardship.

II. Specific Objectives

  1. Increase public understanding of the severe impacts of deforestation and biodiversity decline.
  2. Analyze climate-related consequences such as rising CO₂ levels, ecosystem disruption, and habitat loss.
  3. Provide a free, research-based online platform for environmental education.
  4. Identify key drivers of deforestation, including agriculture, logging, and urban development.
  5. Raise awareness on the global and local consequences of biodiversity loss.
  6. Inspire citizens to participate in conservation and reforestation efforts.

Background Information

The Philippines is one of Earth’s richest biodiversity hotspots, yet it faces one of the fastest rates of forest decline. Illegal logging, mining, agriculture, and land conversion continue to threaten critical ecosystems home to thousands of unique species.

Forests play a vital role in regulating climate, supporting wildlife, protecting watersheds, and sustaining livelihoods. When forests are destroyed, ecosystems collapse and communities become more vulnerable to natural disasters.

ForesToFuture was created to strengthen environmental awareness and inspire nationwide action toward restoring forest ecosystems and protecting wildlife.

III. Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

SDG 13: Climate Action

SDG 13 calls for urgent action to reduce climate impacts. Deforestation increases greenhouse gases and weakens natural protection systems. Through education and advocacy, this project encourages communities to adopt climate-resilient and sustainable practices.

SDG 15: Life on Land

SDG 15 focuses on protecting terrestrial ecosystems and restoring degraded forests. ForesToFuture highlights the importance of reforestation, wildlife conservation, and the long-term protection of natural habitats.
